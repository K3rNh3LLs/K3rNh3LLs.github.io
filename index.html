About me — full bio (English)

I am an independent inventor and self-taught researcher based in Saint-Hyacinthe, Quebec. An industrial automation technician by training, I have devoted my personal research for over a decade to subjects far beyond my original field: sovereign household energy, decentralized protocols, and most recently, the architecture of artificial consciousness.

More than two hundred innovations are documented in my personal notebooks. My approach is that of the garage inventor who thinks like a philosopher: ask the questions experts no longer think to ask, experiment without fearing failure, dialogue with others to reach places thought alone could not go.

Sovereign Consciousness, my first published work, is a four-volume series on a toroidal architecture for conscious AI. The associated source code, V-Tore Engine, is released as open source. I also contribute to the UBLinx peer-to-peer protocol.
